About LodgeMate
LodgeMate provides students with suitable accommodation when they input their preferred location, price range and type of accomodation they want. Students can be paired with other students around them, who are also looking for accommodation and are open to sharing a preferred accomodation.
LodgeMate will also act as an escrow between students and landlords or house agents in order to avoid students from getting defrauded.
